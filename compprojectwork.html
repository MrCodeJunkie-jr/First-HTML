Introduction

  • MIS School, located in the heart of Kathmandu, MIS, is a highly regarded institution known for its excellent academic program and diverse student body. Founded in 1978, MIS has quickly become one of the top schools in the country, attracting students from all over Nepal and abroad. The school offers a wide range of academic programs, including science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) courses as well as humanities, arts, and sports. One of the things that sets MIS apart from other schools is its commitment to providing a well-rounded education. In addition to its rigorous academic program, the school also offers a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports teams, music and drama programs, and clubs for students to get involved in. MIS is also known for its strong sense of community. The school places a strong emphasis on building relationships between students, teachers, and staff, and there are numerous opportunities for students to get involved in service projects and other volunteer work. Overall, MIS School is an exceptional institution that provides students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in their academic and personal endeavors.
